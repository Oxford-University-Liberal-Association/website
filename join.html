Join our ranks!

HOW?

  1. Check Eligibility
  2. Click here for the Official OSLA Square Membership Portal. Our standard membership rate is £8.00 per 1 x Oxford University Academic Year (Michaelmas, Hilary, Trinity) OR Life Membership at £15.00
  3. Purchase the desired membership tier
  4. You will receive an email from us / Square on behalf of us confirming your membership

The OSLA Eligibility Criteria

To join OSLA, you must be / have been a student of either of the Oxford Universities (the University of Oxford OR the Oxford Brookes University). OSLA's Executive reserves the right to admit non-university members on an individual basis through a majority vote of Closed Council. Please feel free to contact us if you have any queries regarding membership - also consider reviewing our Standing Orders for more information.
Adapted from Michaelmas Term 2024 Governing Documents

... Alumni?

Once OSLA, always OSLA ... we always enjoy hearing from alumni of the Association. OSLA members have gone on to do great things ... some even as Prime Ministers of the United Kingdom! Please click here to go to the Alumni page.

WHY?

Do you enjoy lively debate?

Liquor & Liberalism is OSLA's regular flagship event. Drinks of all kinds are provided at discounted rates for members! In addition to L&L, we regularly host Speaker events ... some even our Alumni. We frequently collaborate with other political societies in Oxford from everything from Mock PMQs to curry nights!

Are you interested in meeting new people?

OSLA attracts members from all across Oxford reading a variety of degrees from PPE to Computer Science. Meet new people you might not have spoken to otherwise !

The beating heart of Liberalism at Oxford

Whether you wish to learn, debate or talk about Liberalism ... or all of the above ... or politics in general ... or campaign for the Liberal Democrats in Oxford, OSLA is the place to be. For enquiries about getting involved, please contact us!

Free party membership for one year

The Association offers reimbursement for the first year of Liberal Democrat party membership fees for Association members. Please reach out to the Treasurer directly or the President's email address for more information.

Adapted from Trinity Term 2025 Governing Documents

Association Rules and Regulations

You can find the current governing documents on the Documents page.

Cyber Security Alert

The Webmaster has been made aware of several recent incidents of phishing attempts targeting OSLA members proporting to be sent by members of the Executive (especially the President).

The Webmaster has investigated and found NO evidence to suggest an internal leak of membership data - all members are reminded that official communications will only originate from email addresses ending in '@oxuniliberals.com' or rarely '{COLLEGE}.ox.ac.uk' (if directly from a Committee member).

Please also check your spam mailbox for emails from OSLA — due to the size of our newsletters they are sometimes incorrectly flagged. They will originate from OSLA via MailChimp.
