The Vice-President Emeritus

The Senior Ex-President

Theo Jupp

St Peter's College

The Senior Ex-President is the person who has most recently left the office of President. They serve as an adviser to the Executive and chairs the Emeritus Committee (which deals with electoral complaints and disciplinary matters). Theo is a Councillor in Summertown, and recently stood as a Parliamentary Candidate in Oxford East.
