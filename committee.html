Responsible for overall management of the Association, promoting liberal values through lively social events, engaging speakers and collaborations with other societies.

The General Committee

The Committee is supported by a wide-range of different position holders, some of whom are elected, and others appointed.

The Electoral Officials

The Association also has a team of Electoral Officials, who organise the elections and disciplinary procedures, and advise members on the Rules.

The Senior Officers

The Senior Officers are the Committee registered with the University Proctors. They oversee the Association and the Junior Officers. They are ultimately accountable for the governance of the Association.

The Senior Member

Dr Stephen Goddard

St John's College

The Senior Member serves as the liaison between the University of Oxford and the Association. They are a University member of staff, and provide guidance and impartial advice.
