Welcome to the Oxford Students Liberal Association!

The Junior President's Welcome

We are Oxford's premier liberal society, and we're always eager to welcome new members. Whether you're a committed liberal or simply fancy meeting new people, engaging in debates, and enjoying brilliant socials, you'll always find a warm welcome with us. OSLA strives to be the very heart of Liberalism at Oxford, regularly hosting an array of distinguished Liberal Democrat speakers and collaborative events with other student political societies.

Throughout this website, brilliantly crafted by our Webmaster, Hayden Williams, you'll discover everything you need to know about our society: from the blog to the membership shop, and naturally the absolutely essential term card! Committee members are always delighted to hear from new and prospective members, so please don't hesitate to get in touch with any of us.

Do come and find us at our flagship Liquor and Liberalism social on Wednesdays and follow our social media for updates on our speaker events, panels, and debates! We're absolutely chuffed that you've found our website, and we do hope to see you at one of our events soon.

Yours Liberally,

Theo Cunningham

The Junior President - St. John's College
