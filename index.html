Welcome from The President

Welcome to the Oxford University Liberal Association!

We are Oxford's foremost Liberal Society- and are always keen to have new members. Whether you consider yourself to be an ardent Liberal or are simply interested in meeting new people and enjoying some of our lively debates and numerous socials - you will always be welcome with us. OULA aims to be the beating heart of Liberalism at the University, regularly hosting prominent Liberal Democrat speakers and joint events with the other student political societies.

Throughout this website, fabulously put together by our Outreach Officer Hayden Williams, you will find everything you need about our society; from the termly magazine to the membership form - and of course the all important termcard! Members of the Committee are always keen to hear from new and prospective members so feel free to reach out to any one of us. We're delighted that you've found our website, and we hope to see you at one of our events soon.

Yours Liberally,

Zagham Farhan

The President - University College

Michaelmas Term 2024
